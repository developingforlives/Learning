I am Gillian O’Gorman.

My story is like so many others, maybe even yours. I spent years waking up every morning thinking and feeling like something was missing from my life. I was unaware that my mind was programmed to focus on what I lacked as opposed to feeling gratitude for what I had. I was trapped in my lack mind-set, missing so many opportunities to experience joy and celebrate the successes in my life therefore preventing me from achieving my true potential.

I have since learned that we have up to 70,000 thoughts a day, that’s an average of 3,000 thoughts per hour. So before I left my apartment each morning, I had 3,000 unkind and negative thoughts about myself and my life, incredible when you think of it! Looking back, it’s no wonder I often felt sad, stressed and overwhelmed, and why repeat negative patterns were appearing in my life. What I know now is that our thoughts are creative, the more we THINK and BELIEVE a negative thought over and over, the more negative we feel, thus aligning our energy to negative outcomes.  Work with me and start to align your thoughts, feelings and actions to create positive results in all areas of your life.
