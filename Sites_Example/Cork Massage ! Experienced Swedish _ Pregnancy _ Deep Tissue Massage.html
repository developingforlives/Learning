FAQ

Most frequent questions and answers

Is Massage Good For Lower Back Pain ?

Research shows that massage therapy has several potential health benefits for back pain sufferers, including: Increased blood flow and circulation, which brings needed nutrition to muscles and tissues.This muscle relaxation can improve flexibility, reduce pain caused by tight muscles and even improve sleep.

What are the benefits of deep tissue massage?

Unlike other massage techniques that focus on relaxation, deep tissue massage helps to treat muscle pain and improve stiffness. But it can still help to you unwind mentally, too.

Should I get a 60 or 90 minute massage?

A Client who get a 90 minute massage get more of their body worked upon. Receives a better massage and better results
Depending on your needs. You really can’t go wrong with a 90 min massage esspecially if you have a lot to work on.

What should I expect during my first massage ?

I  will conduct a consultation with you prior to your first treatment.  This will help to plan the treatment to best meet your needs. It is very important for me to understand the reasons for your pain, injury, limited mobility or health concerns so i can get to work in the fastest way possible and in the most appropriate way for you.

What can I expect from a maternity massage ?

Prenatal massage can relieve back pain and improve sleep and mood, as well as decrease stress and reduce labor complications. Prenatal massage is best given while you lie on your side propped up by pillows.
